YOLO v8 Formatting

Bounding boxes must be stored in standardized text format mapping to image coordinates.

[class_id] [x_center] [y_center] [width] [height]
  • All values are normalized [0.0 - 1.0] relative to image width and height.
  • 0: Sesame (target crop)
  • 1: Weed (target spray zone)

How It Works: Smart Spraying Loop

1. Scan & Capture
RGB sensors capture high-speed downward feed.
2. Detect (HSV & Shape)
ECU segments green shapes, separating crops from weeds.
3. Time & Delay
Calculates speed delay to spray exactly as weed hits boom.
4. Solenoid Actuate
Triggers 12V valve to open specific nozzle channel.
5. Spray Target
Nozzles mist weed, leaving nearby crop dry.
